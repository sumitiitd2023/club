ABOUT CLUB

Bharat Electronics Officers' Club (BEOC)

Location: BEL Township, Jalahalli, Bangalore - 560013

Established: 1963

The Bharat Electronics Officers' Club (BEOC) was formed with the aim of providing physical and cultural needs for the officers of Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) and their families. The club's foremost objective is to promote camaraderie and fellowship among its members.

*** Aims of BEOC ***

  • To provide facilities to its members in sports, gym, swimming pool, cultural, social, and recreational activities.
  • To maintain a library of books, periodicals, and literature on cultural topics.
  • To provide facilities for members and management parties.

*** Membership Eligibility ***

Every executive who is on the rolls of M/s. BEL in Bangalore Complex, Corporate Office, Management Block, PDIC, and CRL is eligible to become an ordinary member of the club. Spouses and children of ordinary members are entitled to avail the facilities of the club on par with the ordinary members. Retired executives from BEL are also entitled to become members.

*** Patrons ***

The Director of Bangalore Complex is the patron of the club, and the Chairman and Managing Director of BEL is the Patron-in-Chief.
