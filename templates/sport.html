Sports

Bharat Electronics Officers' Club boasts an impressive range of sports facilities, including tennis, squash, table tennis, badminton, and snooker. The club also features a well-equipped gym and a large swimming pool. Members can participate in yoga, karate, and carom classes. Additionally, the club hosts competitive sports tournaments for its members from time to time.
